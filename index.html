Meet Setsoto Hlohlomi


Lesotho born and raised, I am a vibrant young woman who is passionate about technology and effecting change in the community through my skills and beyond. It brings me joy to put a smile on someone's face, no matter how small the deed.

I am a techie, specializing in IT support as a certified Microsoft Certified Professional and Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate although I am also skilled in Website development. I am constantly intrigued by how different aspects of technology are revolutionizing how we live, work and play.

In the near furure I will put everything on hold and travel - to discover, to learn. Until then, I will be striving to drive change, to touch lives and to be a better human being as our energy often influences those around us.
